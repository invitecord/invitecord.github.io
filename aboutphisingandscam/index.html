Fake Nitro Gifts

The most common scam. You receive a DM with a link like dlscord-glft.com. It looks real, but it's designed to steal your token.

Red Flag

Official links ONLY use discord.gift.

"Hey, can you test my new game?" They send a .zip or .exe file that contains a "token grabber" to hijack your session.
